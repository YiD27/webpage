Project #6 Zoo Princess (Game)

Description

"You look like a monkey!" I heard a girl been mocked by other kids with this line that day. I wasn't brave enough with a good idea to help her at that moment, but I do felt monkeys are beautiful, and there could be a pride here to share similarity with another crature. So I made this another kind princess makeup game based on p5.js.

Design Process

I finished most of the scenes and character illustraion within Adobe Illustrator, and simple loaded into p5 as images resources. The coding logic mainly used "class" as a way to define objects that are treatable in the interaction process. And I addded different game results according to different decisions made by game playrs, though using pretty simple judgement for now, the point is to communicate the idea a prize of able to communicating with animals with similar faces to them, saying its not a shame but a pride.

Reflections

This is the first game attempt I made. Its concept is a consistency of my other works concerning faces and people makeups as tools.
