Project #2 Random Dragon Faces

Description

Developed during Chinese New Year, I built this random dragon faces generator based on a parametric drawing code corresponding to mouse movements. With clicking you can also save the dragon image.

Design Process

I started choosing the color set based on New Year colors and sketching a dragon face to basic geometric patterns. And I put the original geometric-originated face onto a grid system, see how the coordinates can change to generate different faces. Within the math process, I calculated the (x,y) value range of those dots that are leading the change. Then I worked on the mouse movements interaction by divded the p canvas into x direction sections and y direction secions, moving across the X section resulted an eye change and y section resulted into mouse opening and closing. Last but not lease, adding backfround decorations and text detials.

Reflections

In this practice, I mainly explored working on math with computer, and let computer do the most calclation steps proved to be more efficient.
